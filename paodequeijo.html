Pao de Queijo

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F

  2. Pour water, milk, and salt into a large saucepan and place over the heat. When the mixture starts to boil, remove it, and stir in tapioca flour and garlic. Set aside to rest for 15 minutes.

  3. Stir the cheese and egg into the tapioca mixture. Cup-sized balls of the mixture onto an ungreased baking sheet.

  4. Bake in oven for 15 to 20 minutes.

  5. Enjoy!
